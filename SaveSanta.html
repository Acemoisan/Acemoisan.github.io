Save Santa

What is Save Santa?
Save Santa is a puzzle/escape room game. This is the 1st Game Jam I have taken place in. This was part of the Brackeys Game Jam. It is available to be played on Windows and MAC! On Itch.io, the Elixir platform and coming soon to Steam. This game has Play to Earn functionality so that users can earn rewards while they solve puzzles!
I created this game by myself in 7 days. I have created all models and animations myself, in Blender. All programming in Unity, using C#. The entire story. All VFX. All UI and 2D art. Most of the SFX. The Trailer and clips for the game!

In "Save Santa" you play as 3 elves on a quest to Save Santa! Santa was texting and driving and hit a mountain! His sleigh pieces were scattered across the north pole. These unique elves are the only ones that have what it takes to help Santa and save Christmas!
But!
They're trapped! Each elf must escape their confines before they can reach Santa.
In "Save Santa" You can use simple controls to switch between elves, communicate with eachother, and solve puzzles to escape! Not every answer will be in your elf's room so it is important to communicate between elves and search for clues! Once all elves escape, they can be used simultaneously to complete additional puzzles to save Santa!

Find Santa. Repair his Sleigh, and Save Christmas!
