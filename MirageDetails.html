What is Mirage?
Mirage is an open world survival game that I created with a team of 3 people in 7 Days. This is the 2nd Game Jam I have taken place in. This was part of the Brackeys Game Jam. It is available to be played in Browser!
My job in this Game Jam started off as the 3d Modeler and Rigger! I created all of the models and animations that you will see in game, using Blender and Unity! As the Game Jam progressed I took on another role as programmer. Adding many of the in game features and tools.

In "Mirage" you spawn in a desert and must scavenge your environment for food, water, useful tools/weapons and more. To aid your survival. As you become more hungry and thirsty your stats drop and you begin to see Mirages spawn around you. These Mirages can be harsh, or helpful! The simple loop of this game is to survive as long as you can! The timer starts when you spawn, as time goes on, more enemies and tools will spawn in your environment. How long will you last? And how much will you be able to explore?
