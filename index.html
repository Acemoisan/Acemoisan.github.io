About Me

Greetings! My name is Aidan, I am a versatile Canadian artist and programmer, fervently dedicated to the multifaceted world of Game Development.
My expertise shines in Unity development, where I blend programming prowess with artistic creativity.

My proficiency spans the entire development spectrum - Coding, Art, Animating, Music, Design, Testing, Recording, and even Monetizing - ensuring a holistic approach to game creation.
From Mystical Spellcasters to Wandering Undead and Aerial Beings, I delight in the challenge of bringing my own animated characters and objects to life, instilling them with personality and purpose. My skill set allows me to craft not just digital entities, but vibrant, interactive worlds.

My approach is not just about creating; it's about innovating, engaging, and transforming every aspect of game development into an art form.
