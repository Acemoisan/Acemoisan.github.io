About

Hello my name is Aidan, I am a Canadian artist and programmer. I enjoy bringing art to life in the form of Game Development.
I am self taught and have gained the majority of my experience through online courses and assignments, taking part in game jams, collaborating with businesses, and working on my own longterm projects!
I feel most comfortable with 3D programs such as Unity, Unreal, Blender and Maya!
Modeling objects in my environment and giving them my own touch. Leaves me with a great feeling of pride.
I like rigging and animating anything! From my little low poly zombie characters, to flying creatures and simple household objects and mechanics.
My passion is 3D art. I enjoy the entire 3D process. Starting from a cube, and turning it into a fully rigged, animated, and colored character.
I admire the practice of giving characters and objects utility and personification.
It is super fun and rewarding to have a 3D object of my own that I can pass between my various projects, share with others, and 3D print at home!
